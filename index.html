Hi! We're John and Kat

Hey, we're John and Kat! At Skills Lab, we start conversations about concepts while learning new skills and sharing them with others. With real-life meetups in Köln and Düsseldorf (Germany), podcasts with insightful guests, and engaging video tutorials, The Skills Lab aims to connect people who love learning and building things together.

As we prepare ourselves for the new AI-powered era, we explore AI-related skills like building chatbots or using machine learning to solve real-life problems. At the same time we celebrate the things that humans are still best at, such as crafts, hands-on projects, painting, DIY or gardening. Let's connect, learn, and build together!

The Skills Lab Meetups

Our meetups began as casual gatherings on porches or in garages, where we shared our passion for learning and building together. Recognizing the potential to connect with even more like-minded people, we've decided to expand our reach through Meetup. Join us and experience the excitement of exchanging ideas, collaborating on projects, and learning from one another in a welcoming, informal setting.

Tutorials and a podcast

Soon, we'll be releasing a comprehensive tutorial that will guide you step-by-step in building your own customized chatbot, complete with all the necessary code. Additionally, keep an eye out for an upcoming podcast that delves into large language models, exploring high-level concepts and controversies surrounding these powerful AI tools. Stay tuned and join us on this exciting journey of discovery and skill-building!
